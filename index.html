IGNITE Pathways

Important: Team Membership Required

In order to use this website, you must be part of a FIRST team. If you're a coach or administrator, create a new team. If you're a member or mentor, join an existing team.

Platform Benefits

  • Team-to-Team Support: Direct connection between teams for part sharing
  • Sustainable Robotics: Reduce waste by reusing parts
  • Cost Reduction: Access needed parts without purchasing new
  • Points System: Coming soon - TBD
  • Community Building: Foster connections between FIRST teams

How to Get Started

  1. 1. Click "Create Team" below
  2. 2. Provide your coach/administrator information
  3. 3. Create your team profile
  4. 4. Share team details with members

  1. 1. Click "Join Team" below
  2. 2. Enter your team number, name, and team password
  3. 3. Provide your personal information (name, email, role)
  4. 4. You'll be immediately added to the team with access to all features

  • • View your donations and requests in Profile
  • • Track your team's points on the Leaderboard
  • • Mark requests as fulfilled when parts are received
